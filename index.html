Our Story

Circle Circle Dot Dot

Now we have our Cootie Shots! Lauren and Michael met in Mrs. Artsis' homeroom (Green Advisory) at Sierra Canyon, back when cooties were a thing. Although they shared a homeroom, they didn't really interact. They probably should have taken it as a sign when, in the year they were there, Green Advisory won Color Wars for the first and only time in its history to date!

High School

Both Lauren and Michael transferred to Viewpoint School, where they continued to be impervious to the other's existence. Although you might not know it, Lauren was pretty shy in high school, and preferred to hang out with a small group of close friends from her grade. Michael was much more outgoing and could often be found cracking jokes in the lunch area, inviting himself to his English teacher's wedding, or sporting a brightly colored mesh (yes, mesh) Hawaiian shirt.

The Wonder Years

Doing that thing you do. Both Lauren and Michael attended the University of Southern California, where they majored in Psychology. Do you see a pattern? Michael would like to take this opportunity to assert his hypothesis that Lauren followed him from school to school, secretly admiring him from afar. Lauren doesn't quite remember it that way. Rather, she remembers Michael occasionally shouting "Hi!" from across the quad, sporting a ridiculous looking Jew fro and braces - Totally hot.

First Date

After a random Facebook chat conversation, which resulted in a date (Lauren and Michael squabble over who asked who out), Lauren was surprised when a trim, braces-less, short-haired man showed up outside her apartment to take her to a chic Argentinian restaurant in Hollywood.

Meet the Parents

Lauren met Michael's parents at a summer BBQ. Little did Lauren know, Michael told his parents his girlfriend would be attending the BBQ about 30 minutes before Lauren walked in the door. He also told them not to ask questions. Lauren and Michael's parents said about five words to each other that first meeting, and Lauren thought they hated her! In a similar turn of events, Michael met Lauren's dad at a birthday dinner for Lauren's sister held at The Bazaar in Beverly Hills. At the time, Michael was not an adventurous eater like the Ford clan is. Nonetheless, he endured the creative - if bizarre - culinary creations of Jose Andres like a champ.

Wanderlust

Lauren and Michael look for any excuse to travel to far off places. Bonus points if there is good food, picturesque landscapes, and castles involved. Land and Michael learned a lot about each other on this trip. Lauren learned that Michael can never take too many panoramic pictures, has an extremely relaxed sense of time, and is very good at communicating in nonverbals when a language barrier exists. Michael learned that Lauren requires a generous time buffer when traveling in order to maintain sanity, loves to experience different cultures by wondering around the city and eating good food, and is "an outgoing introvert"

Living in Sin

As they prepared to move in together, they had a lot of decisions to make. It's a good thing they're both so good at visualizing how the home would end up looking after its complete remodel! Both Lauren and Michael are excited and grateful to be living in the house that Michael's mom grew up in.
